My Top 5 Favourite Movies

  1. Interstellar
    • Emotional father–daughter story
    • Mind-blowing science and space visuals
    • Beautiful background music by Hans Zimmer
  2. The Dark Knight
    • Heath Ledger’s legendary Joker performance
    • Strong storyline and intense action
    • Deep themes about morality and chaos
  3. Inception
    • Unique storyline about dreams within dreams
    • Excellent direction by Christopher Nolan
    • Thrilling visuals and suspense
  4. Baahubali: The Beginning
    • Epic visuals and grand storytelling
    • Powerful characters and emotional scenes
    • Iconic soundtrack and background score
  5. 96 (Tamil)
    • Beautiful and emotional love story
    • Vijay Sethupathi and Trisha’s natural performances
    • Soul-touching music by Govind Vasantha
